# GMB-Optimization-Local-SEO
Google My Business (GMB) Optimization for Local SEO – A digital marketing capstone project focused on improving the local search visibility of a pharmacy business through Google Business Profile analysis, keyword research, content optimization, local SEO recommendations, and business profile evaluation.

# Google My Business (GMB) Optimization for Local SEO

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing and optimizing a Google Business Profile (GMB) to improve local search visibility and online presence.

## Business Details

* Business Name: Sri Vigneswara Pharmacy
* Category: Pharmacy
* Location: Kotapalli, Andhra Pradesh

## Project Objectives

* Create and analyze a Google Business Profile
* Perform keyword research
* Evaluate local SEO opportunities
* Improve business visibility in local search
* Recommend optimization strategies

## Activities Performed

* Business profile analysis
* Keyword research
* GMB profile evaluation
* Local SEO recommendations
* Visibility assessment

## Keywords Used

* Pharmacy in Kotapalli
* Medical Store in Kotapalli
* Pharmacy Near Me
* Medical Shop Near Me
* Healthcare Products
* Prescription Medicines

## Recommendations

* Add more business photos
* Encourage customer reviews
* Maintain accurate business information
* Improve profile completeness

## Outcome

The project demonstrated how Google Business Profile optimization can improve local search visibility, customer engagement, and online presence for local businesses.
